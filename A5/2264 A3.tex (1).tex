      \hspace{1em} b. ${(0.2x - \frac{1}{13})}^7 = {(12x - 3)}^6$ \vspace{.5em}

      \hspace{1em} c. $(0.2x - \frac{1}{13}) + 4 = 123(12x - 3) - 4$
